Cliffed

How To Play

Press ⬅️ or ➡️ to move left or right.
Use "-" to turn music on/off.
If you hit the top, you will die.
If you hit the bottom without standing on any block, you will die.
Blocks:

You will die if you land on this block

It will bounce you up a little.

When you land on this block, it will disappear after a while

Try to survive as long as you can. Feel free to put your name and score on the leaderboard.
